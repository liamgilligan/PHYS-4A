$$m_1 g - T = m_1 a$$
$$m_1 g - m_2 a = m_1 a$$

$$a = \frac{7}{22}m/s^2 \approx 0.32 m/s^2$$
$$\boxed{a =  0.32 m/s^2}$$

$$\Delta x = (0m/s)t + \frac{1}{2}at^2$$

$$t = \sqrt{\frac{2\Delta x}{a}}$$
$$t = \sqrt{\frac{2 \cdot 0.908 m}{\frac{7}{22}m/s^2}}$$
